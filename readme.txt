Rules
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 - Enter the arena either from the town or by visiting any arena on the adventure map.
 - Select an arena and start the fight. If you win, you will receive a reward.
 - You cannot lose any troops, commanders or henchmen in the arena. Everything will be restored after combat.
 - To challenge the boss battles, you need to activate the Advanced Classes Mod because the rewards were specifically designed for the ACM.
